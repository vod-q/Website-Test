About Goofy Goober Industries

Goofy Goober Industries (GGI) is a world leader in tech innovation and manufacturing. Established in 1954, we continue to lead the way in groundbreaking inventions and cutting-edge machinery.

We are Goobers, proud of our quirky yet high-performing machines and processes.
