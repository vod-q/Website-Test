Our Story

The Legend of Goofy Goober Industries

Once upon a time in a small garage, the dream of creating groundbreaking technology was born. Through determination, innovation, and a little bit of goofiness, Goofy Goober Industries emerged as a tech leader.


The Goofy Goober Industries (GGI) is a major team and primary antagonist in the IGoByLotOfNames GregTech cinematic universe. It was initially founded in late 2022 by EnderReaper64 as a minor player in the Project Towny event. Since then, the GGI has branched out to over a hundred members, and gained major military and economic sway in the Gregic political landscape.


Our lore spans decades of challenges, victories, and endless curiosity. Join us on this adventure as we continue to shape the future of tech.
